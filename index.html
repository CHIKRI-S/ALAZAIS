ALAZAÏS

Explore. Understand. Preserve.

The Alazaïs Agency is an inter-universal organization responsible for the exploration, discovery, and colonization of derived universes. It operates across military, scientific, political, industrial, and economic sectors within the universes under its jurisdiction. The Agency ensures the security of inter-universal transit routes and conducts the neutralization of human, liminal, and subliminal threats.

Throughout history, humanity has imagined other worlds beyond its own.

Carl Sagan said

"Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known."

Since 1905, the Alazaïs Agency has been dedicated to exploring, studying and securing access to newly discovered worlds.

Welcome to the Universes page

Do you know that the number of known universes is constantly increasing?

Indeed, to date, the Agency has discovered over 1,400,200,000 universes, and this number continues to grow as we expand our exploration efforts.

The Department of Worlds Exploration compiles a list of these universes as they are discovered. It first records our bases and the planets on which they are located. It should be noted, first and foremost, that the Agency does not operate across all derived universes, due to limited human and material resources. The Agency’s primary objective within these universes is the study of civilizations—or the remnants of civilizations—discovered on certain planets or satellites scattered among the billions of galaxies within these universes.

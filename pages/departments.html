DEPARTMENTS

The Agency is structured into multiple departments, each dedicated to a specific field of operation.

The Alazaïs Agency has numerous departments that allow it to diversify its activities. These divisions range from simple facility maintenance to the construction of portals and the assault of strategic sites. It should be noted that the Agency occasionally works in collaboration with state, military, civilian, and scientific institutions.

The Agency’s departments are divided into categories, as follows:

Exploration

  • DWE : Department of Worlds Exploration

Science and research

  • DMS : Department of Multidisciplinary Science
  • DLE : Department of Liminality Engineering

Industry and technology

  • DIM : Department of Industrial and Military development

Energy

  • DGE : Department of Global Energy
  • DMR : Department of Material Ressources

Military

  • DAF : Department of Armed Forces
  • DCF : Department of Civilian Forces

Investigation

  • DIS : Department of Internal Security
  • DII : Departments of Internal Intelligence

Archives and memories

  • DDA : Department of Documented Archives
